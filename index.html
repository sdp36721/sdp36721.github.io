SK Telecom

SK Telecom or SKT is known for their amazing accomplishments completed in League of Legends. This professional South Korean team has competed in many League of Legends championships. At the moment, SK Telecom holds the highest record of wins, with three world championship wins. They have also won first place in the Mid-Season Invitational, IEM- World Championship, League of Legends Champions Korea, and All-Star event. SKT's most popular player is Lee Sang-hyeok or more commonly known as his in-game name "Faker". In the game, he plays the role of mid lane which generally deals with mages or assassins. He is famous for his aggresive playstyle and thinks of his fellow team mates as his second family. Faker has been with SKT since the beginning of his pro-years, but that has changed now. Currently at the end of Worlds Championship 2018, Faker has become a new agent and people are do not know if he will conitnue to stay with SK Telecom.
